BinGo App

BinGo is an interactive app that turns recycling into a fun, rewarding, and educational experience. Through bite-sized quizzes, engaging lessons, and a points-based reward system, users can test their recycling knowledge, track progress, and earn real-world perks, from reusable items to community recognition. The design process involved researching user behaviors and pain points, ideating gamified solutions, prototyping intuitive interfaces, and refining interactions through usability testing.
