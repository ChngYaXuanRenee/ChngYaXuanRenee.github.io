P1US App - For People With Visual Disabilities

P1US is an inclusive public transport app designed to improve accessibility and independence for all commuters, especially individuals with visual impairments, mobility challenges, and other accessibility needs. By integrating essential features such as real-time bus and MRT arrival notifications, voice feedback, and a seamless card top-up system, P1US ensures that navigating public transport is smoother, safer, and more intuitive.
