BINGO App - Turning Waste into Wins!

My design process for BinGo was guided by a user-centered approach, ensuring that the app is engaging, educational, and rewarding. I started with thorough research, conducting a Recycling Awareness & App Development Survey to understand user habits, pain points, and motivations. By analyzing competitive apps, I identified gaps and opportunities to create a more effective and enjoyable experience. I also explored gamification strategies and behavioral incentives to encourage sustainable actions.

With these insights, I developed the core concept of BinGo, focusing on quizzes, interactive lessons, and a reward system to drive engagement. I created a user persona to represent the target audience, ensuring that the app addresses real needs. From there, I mapped out the user journey, designing an intuitive flow that smoothly guides users from learning about recycling to earning rewards. I crafted wireframes for key screens, including the home page, quiz check-ins, side quests, and interactive lessons, while designing an onboarding experience that welcomes new users seamlessly.

As I moved toward implementation, I developed a beta version for early adopters and explored potential partnerships with government agencies and sustainability organizations to offer real-world perks. A marketing campaign was planned to encourage adoption and community participation. Throughout this process, my goal remained clear: to create an app that turns waste into wins, making recycling an engaging and rewarding habit for users.
