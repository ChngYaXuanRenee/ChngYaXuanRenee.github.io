Karu Saves Jalan Besar Interactive Map

I’m designing an interactive map to make exploring Jalan Besar fun and engaging. The map features a character called “Jalan Besar”, whose job is to restore and bring the area to life. Visitors join him on his mission, turning a simple walk into an interactive adventure.

The map includes a comic introducing Jalan Besar and his story, and a trail around Jalan Besar where visitors can collect stickers of the places they visit. Each sticker marks a milestone on the trail, making the exploration personal and playful.

At the final location, there’s a QR code that visitors can scan to unlock a celebratory AR animation, bringing Jalan Besar’s restoration story to life and rewarding those who complete the trail.

This project lets me combine storytelling, play, and digital technology to create a fun, memorable, and educational experience that connects people to Jalan Besar in a new way.
