Nostalgic Noodles with AI workflows (ComfyICU). Oct 2024

Designed with AI Comfy.icu, Adobe After Effects, Resolume VJ, Adobe Aero.

AI Integration:
Comfy.icu AI was instrumental in brainstorming and generating visuals that aligned with the project's theme of nostalgia. By feeding keywords such as "warmth," "tradition," and "family moments," the AI produced stunning, evocative imagery that captured the essence of old-world Singapore.

After Effects:
I added more animation with the mask, animation related to my brand identity and it conveys a sense of nostalgia and comfort, evoking memories of classic noodle dishes while adding a modern touch! The motion and evolving colors of my logo reinforce my brand’s presence and make it instantly recognizable. The flowing linework represent the essence of noodles or the interconnectedness of memories and emotions tied to my brand too!

Resolume VJ software:
For the Nostalgic Noodles project, my experience with VJ software allowed me to explore the dynamic interplay of visuals and storytelling. Using the VJ tools, I crafted visually engaging sequences that reflected the nostalgic theme of the project. I manipulated motion graphics, textures, and patterns to create fluid transitions, emphasizing the warmth and sentimental value of the concept. The software’s real-time rendering capabilities enabled me to experiment with live visual layering, such as overlaying vintage-style graphics with contemporary animations. This process gave me a deeper appreciation for the synergy between visual design and emotional storytelling, as I was able to evoke a sense of nostalgia while maintaining a modern and cohesive aesthetic. Through this, I translated the project’s themes into an immersive visual journey that captivated audiences.

AR integration with Adobe Aero:
AR is a powerful tool that helps me align Nostalgic Noodles’ brand identity with modern expectations. It transforms customer interactions into memorable, engaging experiences while reinforcing the core emotional and cultural values of my brand. Through personalization, fun, and storytelling, AR ensures that my brand leaves a lasting impression and builds stronger connections with my customers.
