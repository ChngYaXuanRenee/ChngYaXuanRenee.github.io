Medium: Adobe Illustrator & Photoshop. Nov 2023.

Challenged myself to design 3 magazine cover pages WITHOUT the use of graphics. Strictly only the usage of letterforms. And limited to 2 types of fonts. This task is designed for me to show the creative usage of type and layout that will be used to amplify a message.

THOUGHT PROCESS: Initially I've successfully edited and modified the letters that looked interesting and layouts that follows the editorial trendy style of 2023. However, I wanted to show my full capabilities to increase the attractiveness and aesthetic capabilities of the designs hence I fully used the applications effects with critical thinking. I also heavily play with gradients to incorporate dynamic ways to enhance the poster in a bold and powerful way.
