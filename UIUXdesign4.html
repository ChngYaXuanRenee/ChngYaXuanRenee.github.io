SPACES REDEFINE Archifest 2025 Rebrand Mobile App Design

The design approach for the Archifest 2024 app and festival assets blends functionality with a visually appealing aesthetic to enrich the user experience. Clear information delivery is balanced with creative design to reflect the "Spaces Redefined" theme effectively.

Landing pages use a dark blue palette with pixelated effects, creating a unified aesthetic. Program pages emphasize essential information while reducing visual clutter. Conference pages label days with pixelated graphics for easy navigation.

Individual program days incorporate dynamic pixelated graphics that overlap and merge, representing interconnected evolving urban spaces. Unique features like interactive 3D models allow users to explore architectural designs from multiple angles, enhancing engagement.

Overall, this app design merges structured information with captivating visuals to create a memorable and practical branded experience for the festival audience.
