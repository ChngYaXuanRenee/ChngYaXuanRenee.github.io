SPACES REDEFINE Archifest 2025 Rebrand Mobile App Design

The design approach for the Archifest 2024 app and festival assets centers on blending functionality with a visually appealing aesthetic to enrich the user experience. By developing assets that balance clear information delivery with creative design, I aim to reflect the "Spaces Redefined" theme dynamically and effectively. Research on Archifest’s existing app provided insights into its strengths, clear information presentation and consistent visual style, as well as areas for improvement, such as adding visual interest and optimizing the information hierarchy.

For the landing page, I established a stronger visual identity by using a dark blue palette with pixelated effects, creating a unified aesthetic that reinforces the event's brand. This visual strategy was applied across the program pages, where the initial design was simplified to emphasize essential information, reducing visual clutter to better reflect my concept.

Similarly, the conference landing page now uses labeled days with pixelated graphics for easy navigation, and program details for each day are organized with clear headings and subheadings for clarity. This organization aids in visually guiding users to relevant information efficiently.

In the design for individual program days, I incorporated a flexible approach to the pixelated graphics, overlapping and merging them to introduce a fluid, dynamic structure that aligns with the "Spaces Redefine" theme. This change enhances the representation of the festival's core concept, conveying a sense of interconnected, evolving urban spaces while maintaining a visually engaging layout.

A unique feature of Dr. Ken Yeang’s landing page includes interactive 3D models that allow users to explore architectural designs from multiple angles, adding depth and a sense of realism. These models create an immersive experience, allowing users to visualize architecture interactively, thus enhancing engagement and aligning with the innovative spirit of the event.

Overall, this app design and asset creation focus on merging structured information with captivating visuals, ensuring a memorable, branded experience that is both practical and aesthetically appealing for the festival audience
