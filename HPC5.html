I manage art direction and designs Sembcorb showcase booth for Singapore International Water Week (SIWW) for Minister of Sustainability, Ms Grace Fu, utility and city leaders.

For the Sembcorp showcase booth at Singapore International Water Week, my design process began with a deep understanding of the brief, focusing on the needs of the target audience, including Minister Grace Fu, utility experts, and city leaders. The primary goal was to communicate Sembcorp’s brand values, sustainability, innovation, and leadership, through a booth that was both visually compelling and highly engaging.

I started by researching Sembcorp’s branding and exploring innovative trends in exhibition design to create a concept centered on water as a theme. Flowing elements symbolized adaptability and sustainability

Collaboration was integral to the process, as I worked closely with stakeholders to ensure the design aligned with Sembcorp’s message while incorporating feedback to refine the final presentation. Attention to detail during setup ensured that all elements, from lighting to finishes—delivered a polished, professional look. The project not only allowed me to showcase my creative and strategic skills but also pushed me to think innovatively about using visuals to capture and hold the attention of a high-profile audience.
