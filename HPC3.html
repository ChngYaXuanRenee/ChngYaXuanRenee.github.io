WINGS OF JOY

My design, “Wings Of Joy” conveys the concept of facilitating the delight of travel. It inspires a sense of freedom, enthusiasm, and positivism, which is consistent with the concept of travel. Furthermore, "Wings of Joy" implies that flying with Scoot not only transports passengers to their destination but also provides them joy and happiness along the way. This idea captures the heart of Scoot's mission, to create a pleasurable travel experience throughout Southeast Asia.

I would like to use the concept of a key as a metaphor for opening up a whole new universe of diverse destinations in Southeast Asia. Through my work, I hope to portray a sense of movement that animates a plot, transporting viewers on a voyage of discovery and exploration. By seeing the key as a portal to new experiences, I hope to generate a sense of excitement and adventure, allowing viewers to go on a visual journey through Southeast Asia's vivid and diverse landscapes. And most importantly to never stop travelling with Scoot!
